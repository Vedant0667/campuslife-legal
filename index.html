In crisis? Help is available right now.

CampusLife is not a crisis or emergency service and no one monitors your entries. If you or someone you know may be in danger or experiencing a mental-health crisis:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline — call or text 988, or chat at chat.988lifeline.org (free, confidential, 24/7, US)
  • Crisis Text Line — text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency — call 911 or your local emergency number
  • Outside the US — find a local helpline at findahelpline.com

Delete your account & data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app: open the Profile tab → Delete account, and confirm. This removes your account, your wellbeing entries, and your family link (including removing your entries from any linked parent's view).

If you can't access the app, you may request deletion by emailing support@campuslife.app from the address associated with your account. We will verify and process the request.
