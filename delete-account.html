Delete your CampusLife account

This page is for the CampusLife mobile app (com.ronaldvedant.campuslife) by an independent developer.

Delete instantly, in the app

1. Open CampusLife and go to the Profile tab.

2. Scroll to Legal & account and tap Delete account.

3. Confirm. Your account is deleted immediately.

Or request deletion without the app

Email campuslifefeedback@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my CampusLife account" from the email address associated with your account. We verify the request and process it within 30 days. You do not need to reinstall the app.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in credentials.
  • All of your wellbeing entries (moods, rankings, notes) — including removing them from any linked parent's view.
  • Your family link, and any device push tokens registered for notifications.
  • If you are the last member of your family, the family itself and its data.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. We do not keep your wellbeing data after deletion; residual copies in encrypted backups roll off within our provider's standard backup retention window.
